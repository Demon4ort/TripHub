Best Locations for Camping in the World


Amsterdam

Amsterdam is one of the most visited cities of Western Europe with countless attractions. The city is home to numerous camping sites that offer varied outdoor accommodation options such as bungalows, tents, caravans and self-catering accommodation. Amsterdam is a must-go city for everyone, so book a plane ticket to Amsterdam and enjoy this beautiful city. Top campsites of Amsterdam include Vliegenbos and Gaasper Camping that are located within fifteen minutes away from the city center. You can take the ferry from Vliegenbos to Central Train Station. On the other hand, Gaasper Camping is easily accessed by train.

Geneva

Geneva is the largest city of Western Switzerland and located on the shores of the amazing Lake Geneva. The city is known for its unique atmosphere and beautiful scenery. Camping in Geneva is one of the best camping experiences you can have and this experience is awaiting you here. Lake Geneva situated at the border between Switzerland France is a famous spot for camping. Haute-Savoie, Publier, Thonon-les-Bains, Lugrin or Messery are home to nice campsites near Geneva. The most comfortable way to travel in the surrounding region is problably by car.

Zurich

Zurich is located in the northern part of Switzerland just next to the Lake Zurich and is a camping haven for nature enthusiasts. Many of the camping sites in Zurich have a direct lakeside location which makes your camping trip refreshing and fun. Book your flight ticket to Zurich not only for a nature trip, but also to enjoy this amazing city. Campsite Am Schützenweiher is a preferred place for camping near Zurich. You can reach there in about half an hour by taking the train to Winthertur and then the bus to Schützenhaus. Campsite Reussbrücke is another great spot located at Ottenbach. It is connected to Zurich central train station within one hour.

Tbilisi

Tbilisi is the capital city of Georgia and located in the eastern part of the country. Its camping sites are mostly around the well-preserved Tbilisi Sea with affordable prices. You can book your flight to Tbilisi and start preparing for a relaxing vacation. You can enjoy the beautiful landscape of Georgia at GeoCamp Tblisi where a bunch of options for campers with caravans or motorhomes are available. Camping tours throughout Georgia are also organized. The best way to reach GeoCamp Tblisi camping ground is probably by personal car.

Almaty

Almaty is the largest city of Kazakhstan and located on the foothills of the famous Kok Tobe. Almaty has amazing camping sites that have a direct view of the city from Kok Tobe. A plane ticket to Almaty is not only a great way to have a nature-filled experience but also the first step to discover the cultural heritage of the city. Trekking Club campsite is located within a natural park in about two hours distance from Almaty. If you are not travelling by car, you can get on a bus to Issyk or Turgen and then take the taxi. The campsite offers everything you will need ranging from fully furnished spacious tents and rentals to excellent buffet meals. Best Locations for Camping in Turkey

Muğla

Muğla is located in the most southwestern part of Turkey and one of the most popular tourism destinations of the country with its worldly recognized districts like Marmaris, Fethiye, and Bodrum. Despite its popularity, the city has many amazing secluded areas suitable for camping. Most of the camping locations in Muğla are also have spectacular sceneries and natural surroundings. Kayaköy Art Camp, Kelebekler Valley, Katrancı Natural Park, Dalyan Camping, Faralya Camping or Aktaş Beach Camping are famous sites to do camping especially during warm summer days. A flight ticket to Muğla is the first step to experience its all-around natural beauties.

İzmir

İzmir is one of the largest and most influential cities of western Turkey. The city is a prominent summer destination with millions of local and international tourists annually. İzmir is also home to many top-notch camping sites and untouched bays in the middle of the nature such as Delikli Koy Camping Site, Ilıca Beach Camping, Çanak Koyu Camping and Kaynaklar Camping. An amazing İzmir experience is awaiting you in here.

Hatay

Hatay is located in the most southern part of Turkey and an underrated tourism destination with its gorgeous beaches, camping locations and cultural heritage. You can choose Samandağ Teknepınarı, Dörtyol Topraktaş Plateau or Belen Güzelyayla for free camping sites nearby Hatay. Esenbahçe Camping Site and Orient Camping are also good alternatives within easy reach from the city center. A flight ticket to Hatay is the perfect first step to a joyful holiday.

Nevşehir

Nevşehir is located in the central part of Turkey and is one of the most-visited global attractions of the country with mostly thanks to Cappadocia. In Nevşehir, there are also many areas to camp freely, in which you can also enjoy the scenery with amazing hot balloon rides during sunrise. Panorama Camping, Göreme Camping Aqua Park and Avanos Ada Camping are pleasant venues to experience outdoor camping with a small fee. A plane ticket to Nevşehir will help you experience this scene firsthand.

Balıkesir

Balıkesir is located in the southern coast of Marmara Sea and is home to many amazing natural parks, lakes and mountains. A Balıkesir plane ticket is a great way to enjoy what the city has to offer ranging from relaxing camping sites to vibrant seaside attractions. Erdek district and Kapıdağ Peninsula are famous locations for camping in the region. Kapıdağ Camping, Erdek Ant Camping, Abant Camping, Mysia Beach Camping and Çamlıkaltı Picnic and Camping are preferred spots in summertime.
